Welcome to the Quiz!

Round 1: Is It In The Room With Us?

Look around you... or maybe don’t. In this round, you’ll be given clever descriptions of objects, items, or concepts that could very well be lurking right here in the room. Some will be obvious, some will be tricky—but all of them are directly connected to our immediate environment or context. Your task is to listen carefully, connect the clues, and identify what is present, what you can see, and what is directly observable in the given context. The answer might even be right under your nose.

Quiz Rules - Round 1

  • You will have 15 seconds for each question.
  • There are a total of 12 questions.
  • Questions pass clockwise. If a team can’t answer, it moves to the next.
  • The team that answers correctly earns the point, and the following question goes to the next team in order.
  • 10 points for correct answer, no negative marking

Round 2: The Birthday Problem

In this round, you’ll be shown a birthday along with a set of carefully chosen images related to the person who was born on that date. Your task is to connect the dots—analyze the date, interpret the images, and figure out who the person is. Think of it as a puzzle where history, culture, and sharp observation skills come together. The faster you link the clues, the closer you’ll be to the right answer.

Quiz Rules - Round 2

  • You will have 15 seconds for each question.
  • There are a total of 12 questions.
  • Questions pass counter-clockwise. If a team can’t answer, it moves to the next.
  • The team that answers correctly earns the point, and the following question goes to the next team in order.
  • 10 points for correct answer, no negative marking

Round 3: The Reflex Hypothesis

This is the speed round—no time to overthink. A variety of quick, intuitive questions will appear on the screen, and it’s all about reflexes. The faster you respond, the better your chances of scoring. It’s first come, first serve, so trust your instincts and be ready to fire your answers without hesitation.

Quiz Rules - Round 3

  • You will have 15 seconds for each question.
  • There are a total of 11 questions.
  • Buzzer will be placed for each team.
  • The team that hits the buzzer first gets to answer.
  • 10 points for correct answer, -5 for wrong answer
